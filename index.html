About me

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Computational Earth Sciences at Stanford University, working on research at the intersection of deep learning and seismology. I hold a Master in Science and Executive Engineering from the Ecole Nationale Superieure des Mines de Paris.

Previously, I was with Google, Chevron, Schlumberger, Actimage, and Valeo. I have moved around, spending time living in the Netherlands, France, Japan, the Philippines, and the United States. Somewhere in there, I found the time to get my helicopter license.
